My Little Pizzeria

"This is my neighborhood go-to pizza place. The slices are awesome, and there's gelato in the back. I see kids and families laughing and smiling all the time and the turnover is great. Service is wonderful, and efficient."

My little Pizzeria is a classic pizza spot on Court Street and for good reason. With Sicilian, Supreme and a large variety of toppings, My Little Pizzeria never gets old.

Antonio's Pizzeria

"I'm both surprised and selfishly relieved that Antonio's doesn't win more New Yorker or Time Out New York awards for having the best pizza in Brooklyn. More for me."

Antonio's is an old style pizza place, renovated and relocated right across from Borough Hall. With a fresh and healthy smoothie bar and variety of baked Italian foods, its great food from great ingredients.
